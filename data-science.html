Important things to consider about B.Sc. Data Science-

B.Sc. Data Science means Bachelor of Science in Data Science. It is a three-year undergraduate degree program that takes into account the basic concepts of statistics, data analysis, and machine learning. This is a full time course and comes under the fields of Business analytics, Artificial intelligence, and Computer Science.
Students of B.Sc. Data Science are made to learn the problem solving skills that are to be used with huge datasets. Want to enroll in this course? Just go through the below mentioned eligibility criteria, future scope and main subjects of this course.

What is the eligibility criteria for doing B.Sc. Data Science?

Students seeking for admission in B.Sc. Data Science must have studied their 10+2 from a reputed school and board with science stream, i.e. with medical or non-medical stream. They must have scored at least 50-60% marks in their 10+2. Students also need to clear the Common Entrance Test (CET) for getting admission in this course.


What are the main subjects in B.Sc. Data Science?

Students of B.Sc. Data Science are made to learn computing, data analytics, accounting, and many other related subjects.
The main subjects of this course include-

  • Probability and inferential statistics
  • Cloud computing
  • Data warehousing
  • Introduction to artificial intelligence
  • Discrete mathematics
  • Object-oriented programming in Java machine learning, and
  • Multidimensional modelling, etc.

Final Thoughts

B.Sc. Data Science comes under the domains of computer science, analytics,and artificial intelligence. So, it offers varied job opportunities from different fields. Thus, students can easily make successful careers after doing this course.
